ABOUT CHIKMAGALUR

Chikmagalur is renowned for its tranquil surroundings, verdant woods, and high mountains. The delicate, fragrant Mysore Coffee Beans are another specialty of the Chikmagalur coffee estate. There is no set time for visiting the plantation, but it is preferable to go when work is in progress. Taking a tour of the resplendent coffee plantations is one of the most essential things to do in Chikmagalur. Aside from reveling in the fresh aromatic smell of coffee beans and relaxing amidst the verdant green plantations, the estates owners arrange frequent tours where you can learn about the history of coffee and other details.
